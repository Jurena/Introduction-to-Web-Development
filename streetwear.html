Skinny Lightwash Denim: Versatile especially during the cooler months. Works best in Palewave outfits. Stick to these or black jeans. There are multiple washes to choose from, but the lighter, the more versatile. Be sure to avoid bluer washes, but also avoid whiter washes, the more neutral between the two, the more versatile. You can purchase various qualities of lightwash denim ranging from budget, mid-tier and raw-denim.
